Nominal Donations: $ 100,000,000

After $100,000,000 of donation, here's how many people we saved. To get our data, this is what we did: First, we got lists of data for the population in different districts in Sierra Leone. While this may not be 100% accurate, it provides a pretty good estimate of how much people need in different places based on population density. Then, we assumed a worker per every 1,000 population and used a complex algorithm to determine where each center should go. The cost of each center is determined by $500 for each worker and $3 for each person helped. As the poverty/income rate for Sierra Leone is just below $3, we thought this would be an appropriate cost.
